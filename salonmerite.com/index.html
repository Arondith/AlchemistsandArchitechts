Welcome to Salon Mérite

Salon Mérite is a unique, boutique & upscale hair salon in Seattle. A true hidden gem located in Madison Park/Miller Park neighborhood. We are a clean, open-air space hair salon in Seattle with tons of natural light and 4 chairs for your safety and comfort. Our hair salon in Madison Park, where free parking is abundant, offers the most personalized and intimate care for you & your hair. Welcome to the best hair salon Seattle! Owned by renowned hair colorist and stylist, Jedediah Roberts.
